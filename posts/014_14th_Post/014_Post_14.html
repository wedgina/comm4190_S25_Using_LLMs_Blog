Can AI replicate the mind of an music producer?

Music
Using text to music and evaluating its similarities to already popular songs
Author

Wedgina Norelus

Published

March 29, 2025

Today I plan to use text to music ai with lyrics from already popular songs to see if the beats will be similar to each other. Loudly is an AI that takes what you describe and creates a personalized song for it a beat to it I used Loudly’s AI and pasted a little bit of the main chorus, or most known lyrics from the songs into Loudly and listened to the beats it decided would use to match the lyrics. I am aware that with a lot of processes of song production, it usually occurs that the artist hears the beat first and then comes up with lyrics for it instead though, but I don’t really care.

The first song I decided to try was ‘Not Like Us’ by Kendrick Lamar. These were the lyrics I pasted from there

I’m finna pass on this body, I’m John Stockton

Beat your ass and hide the Bible if God watchin’

Sometimes you gotta pop out and show niggas

Certified boogeyman, I’m the one that up the score with ’em

I feel the same way about this one. I feel like it is still too fast lol and electric. It’s giving edm. The original song, is giving drummy/guitar, but in the generated beat it is mostly symbals. It sounds so futuristic, but the original sounds so sweet and gives summer-y on the beach vibes.

I think it is obvious how I overall felt over this AI. I think obviously the produced beats were not that similar to the original. But I especially disliked it because its immediate default was just edm or electric vibes and being really fast. Maybe I just needed to give it sadder lyrics. But I don’t know I feel like the lyrics for Birds of A Feather was obviously not meant to have that quick paced rhythm or happy beat but it still produced a beat that was significantly faster than the original. And I feel like when it gets more range and variation within its beats and pace, that’s when it will get more accurate in identifying songs for lyrics because it’ll be less likely to default to that edm, which is not the only type of beat that exists.
